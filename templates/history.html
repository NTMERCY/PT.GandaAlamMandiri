About us / History
PT Ganda Alam Makmur (PT GAM) is a company holding a Foreign Investment Production Operation Mining Business License (IUP OP PMA) located in East Kutai Regency, East Kalimantan Province. PT GAM is currently entering into a corporate joint operation between LX International and PT Titan Mining Indonesia which is engaged in coal mining. PT GAM has started production in September 2016 and has made its first shipment since September 21, 2016. Furthermore, 2017 was the first year in which PT GAM achieved its production target of ± 4,000,000 MT. In 2022, PT GAM has a production target of 15,500,000 MT, which has a Production Operation Permit area of ​​10,600 Ha with a project area of ​​246.45 Ha. PT GAM has made sales to various industrial sectors, both domestically and exports which became a big momentum for PT GAM to succeed in mining activities in the following year .
PT Ganda Alam Makmur (PT GAM)
- SK KP (Kuasa Pertambangan) Eksplorasi of PT Ganda Alam Makmur had been issued (2005)
- SK IUP OP (Operation) of PT Ganda Alam Makmur had been issued (2007)
- LG Internasional 60% share at PT Ganda Alam Makmur (2012)
- PT Ganda Alam Makmur started mining operational (2016)
